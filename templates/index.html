Welcome

Welcome to Dowling's Bar and Grill – where the exhilarating world of sports and the unmistakable New York City vibe come together for an unbeatable experience. As a 3rd generation family-run sports bar, we're more than just a place to watch the game.

Beginning in the heart of the city that never sleeps, Dowling's Bar and Grill embodies the fusion of family tradition and sports passion. Our roots run deep, harking back to the bustling streets of New York where our journey began, John Dowling opened the first Dowling’s in New York City in 1933 after emigrating from Dublin. Across generations, our commitment to serving up great food and unforgettable moments remains unshaken and as brought us back to the shores of Dublin City.

Step inside and soak in the electric atmosphere that mirrors the excitement of a packed stadium. The walls filled with sports memorabilia collected over the years, and the cheers from fellow sports enthusiasts create an ambiance that's as thrilling as being front row in the Aviva as the Irish team walk out or on Hill 16 in Croke Park on All Ireland final weekend. At Dowling's, you're not just watching a game – you're part of a community.

Our menu takes inspiration from some American game day classics and is a diverse mix of classic favourites and modern twists. From hearty burgers to finger-licking wings, every bite is a tribute to the dynamic culinary landscape of New York, with an Irish twist of course.

We look forward to welcoming you to Dowling’s Bar and Grill, whether it’s for the All-Ireland Final or just a Friday night with the boys. Check out our game day menu and book online to avoid disappointment.
