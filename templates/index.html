Welcome

Welcome to Dowling's Bar & Grill. We are located on the old Dublin road in Co. Westmeath. Join us to watch the biggest live sporting events and eat some of the best American inspired food cooked by our award winning inhouse chef. Transport to New York City with our fresh take on the Irish American Sports Bar.


Dowling's Bar & Grill is a brand new sports bar and restaurant in the midlands of Ireland. Our aim is to serve up some of the best American inspired food on the island while showing all live sports daily. We show GAA, Football, NBA, NFL, UFC, WWE, Tennis, Formula 1, Cricket and much much more.. We have over 50 wide screen TV's and cater for all occasions.
